Shamrock Shakes

About the Shamrock Shake

A minty St Patrick's Day favourite.

The Shamrock Shake is a creamy seasonal milkshake that was introduced in 1970.

Ingredients

  • Ice cream and milk
  • Mint extract
  • Food coloring
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Decorating sugar

Step-by-step how to make the shake

  1. Blend the ice cream, milk, extract, and food coloring in a blender
  2. Drizzle the inside of the glass with chocolate syrup
  3. Pour the shake into the glass
